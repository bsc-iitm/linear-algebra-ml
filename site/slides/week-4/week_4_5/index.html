    Summary¶

    ::: {.columns align=left}

    ::: {.column width="100%"}



    ::: incremental

    The following are equivalent definitions of diagonalizablity. A matrix \(A\) is diagonalizable if

    • \(\mathbb{R}^n\) has a basis of eigenvectors of \(A\)
    • \(A\) is similar to a diagonal matrix
